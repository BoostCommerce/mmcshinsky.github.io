World Soccer 5s is the governing body for 5 man soccer. They promote fast paced games that actually end with goals! Working with the WS5 team has been great in bringing their vision and goal for both small leagues and national tournaments to life. With WS5, we focused on signups and education of the game. As proof of concept, World Soccer 5s brings in 1000's of visitors every week and signups never stop for all of their tournaments.
