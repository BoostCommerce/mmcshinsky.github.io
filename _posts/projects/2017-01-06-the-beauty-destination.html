A fashion and beauty site run by Michelle Money & Laura Armstrong promoting education and availability of beauty products throughout the industry. Working with Michelle and Laura was a privilege especially when your clients have their vision and direction already ready for you! The Beauty Destination focuses on Eminence, a 100% organically grown, hand-picked, and hand-stirred product that is full of rich antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals.
